Book Submission Guidelines

📘 Overview

Follow Nova Publisher guidelines strictly.

📄 Format

  • Word (.docx) preferred
  • Times New Roman, 10.5 pt
  • US Letter size

📚 References

  • Chicago / APA / IEEE (consistent)
  • All references must be cited

🖼 Figures

  • Minimum 350 DPI
  • No copyrighted images

Review Process

All papers undergo peer review using Turnitin plagiarism check.

🚫 Plagiarism Policy

ICSTAIS 2026 strictly follows ethical publication practices.

  • More than 10% similarity → Rejected
  • Single source must be < 2%
  • Checked via Turnitin

Attendance

At least one author must present the paper.
